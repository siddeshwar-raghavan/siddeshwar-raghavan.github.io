Research Intern, IIT Bombay
June 2017

Worked on a VSLAM project under the guidance of Prof. Rajbabu Velmurugan and I developed a system to identify markers and distance of the markers from the video captured by a single camera rather than the conventional multi-camera approach. This was achieved by segmenting it into frames and analyzing each frame to find the position of the marker. I had chosen the approach to identify markers from the video feed to make the solution as weakly supervised as possible.
